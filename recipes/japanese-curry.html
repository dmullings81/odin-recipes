Japanese Curry

Description

This is a simple recipe for Japanese curry. It is healthy, easy to make, can be eaten for a at least two days, and most importantly tastes great!
Makes enough for 4 people at least.

  • Curry roux
  • 400g of meat - sliced pork, beef, or even chicken, but any cut is OK
  • 2-3 onions (sliced)
  • 1 carrot (diced)
  • 3 medium potatoes (diced and soaked in water)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  1. Fry onions and garlic for ages (20m+) stirring continuously until very soft. Or microwave.
  2. Add + fry meat until browned.
  3. Stir in carrots.
  4. Stir in potatoes.
  5. Add water until it covers everything.
  6. Bring to the boil and cover.
  7. Cook until potatoes and carrots are cooked (15-20m usually). Be sure to take off any scum.
  8. Lower heat and add the curry roux (whole box). Wait for it to melt then stir in.
  9. Cook for 5m stirring occasionally.
  10. Consistency should be a bit gravy-like, but depending on preference you can add more water.
